# Xenaia

Customer operations automation for reservation-driven businesses (tours,
activities, rentals, classes, appointments). Xenaia connects to your
helpdesk, live chat, and booking system, then automates ticket triage,
AI-drafted answers grounded in your own content, availability sync,
support-quality evaluation, and staff rostering.

Single-tenant and self-hosted: every deployment serves one business, and
everything business-specific lives in configuration, data, and content
packs, never in code. Examples throughout use the fictional demo tenant
"Meridian Trails".

Status: early development. See [docs/architecture.md](docs/architecture.md)
and [docs/conventions.md](docs/conventions.md).
